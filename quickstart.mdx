# Quickstart Guide

This guide will help you get started with Budget Bee quickly. You'll learn how to create an account, add your first transaction, and explore the dashboard.

## Step 1: Create Your Account

Budget Bee supports two authentication methods:

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Email & Password">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Navigate to Sign Up">
        Visit the Budget Bee application and click on **Sign Up** or **Register**.
      </Step>
      
      <Step title="Fill in Your Details">
        Enter your information:
        - **Name**: Your full name (minimum 2 characters)
        - **Email**: A valid email address
        - **Password**: Must meet these requirements:
          - At least 8 characters
          - At least 1 lowercase letter
          - At least 1 uppercase letter
          - At least 1 number
      </Step>
      
      <Step title="Accept Terms">
        Check the box to agree to the Terms and Conditions.
      </Step>
      
      <Step title="Verify Your Email">
        After signing up, you'll receive a verification email. Click the link in the email to verify your account.
        
        <Warning>
          You must verify your email before you can access Budget Bee features.
        </Warning>
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Google Sign-In">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Click Google Sign-In">
        On the login or sign-up page, click the **Sign in with Google** button.
      </Step>
      
      <Step title="Select Your Account">
        Choose the Google account you want to use for Budget Bee.
      </Step>
      
      <Step title="Grant Permissions">
        Allow Budget Bee to access your basic profile information.
      </Step>
      
      <Step title="Complete Setup">
        You'll be automatically signed in and redirected to the transactions page.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Step 2: Add Your First Transaction

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Transactions">
    After logging in, you'll be on the transactions page by default, or click **Transactions** in the sidebar.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Create a Transaction">
    Click the **+** button or use the keyboard shortcut to open the transaction dialog.
    
    Fill in the transaction details:
    - **Name**: Description of the transaction (e.g., "Coffee at Starbucks")
    - **Amount**: The transaction amount
    - **Currency**: Select from supported currencies (default: USD)
    - **Date**: When the transaction occurred
    - **Category**: Choose from default categories (Food, Travel, Sales) or create your own
    - **Status**: Paid, pending, or other status
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Save the Transaction">
    Click **Save** to create your transaction. It will appear in your transactions list immediately.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Budget Bee automatically creates three default categories when you sign up: Food, Travel, and Sales.
</Tip>

## Step 3: Explore the Dashboard

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Dashboards">
    Click **Dashboards** in the sidebar to view your dashboards.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Create a Dashboard">
    Click **Create Dashboard** to build your first custom view.
    
    Give your dashboard a name (e.g., "Monthly Overview").
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Add Widgets">
    Once created, you can add various widgets:
    - **Number Cards**: Display single metrics (total income, expenses, etc.)
    - **Bar Charts**: Visualize data over time
    - **Line Charts**: Track trends
    - **Donut Charts**: See category breakdowns
    
    Widgets are fully customizable and can be rearranged using drag-and-drop.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Step 4: Set Up Categories

Categories help you organize your transactions:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Category Management">
    Categories can be managed from the transaction creation dialog or settings.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Create Custom Categories">
    Click **New Category** and provide:
    - **Name**: Category name
    - **Description**: Optional description
    - **Color**: Choose a color for visual identification
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Assign to Transactions">
    When creating or editing transactions, select the appropriate category from the dropdown.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Keyboard Shortcuts

Budget Bee supports keyboard shortcuts for faster navigation. Navigate to Settings → Shortcuts to view all available keyboard shortcuts, including:

- `Shift + H` - Navigate to home
- `Shift + T` - Navigate to transactions
- `Shift + X` - Navigate to settings
- `Shift + A` - Navigate to accounts
- `N` - Open new transaction dialog

<Note>
  Some features like subscriptions may require a paid subscription plan. Check your account status in settings.
</Note>
